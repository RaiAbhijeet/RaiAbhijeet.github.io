About

01 / background

I spend my time at an unusual seam: the semantic data models that describe a machine, and the spatial interfaces a technician actually uses to see it. Most people work one side of that line. I work the bridge.

Built real AR systems for aerospace and rail clients at Accenture — CAD overlays, on-site interactive experiences. My Master's thesis at Fraunhofer IGCV tackled mixed reality for collaborative factory planning. Lately I've been wiring LLMs into that world: local, DSGVO-aware, on-premise where the data can't leave the building.
